A Tribute to Casablanca

It began with a simple question: In a world of instant gratification, what remains? The answer was found in the winding alleys of the old medina, where artisans still work with the rhythm of their breath, not the ticking of a clock.

NOUR & Co. was established to preserve these dying arts. Whether it is the distillation of rare Oud that takes thirty years to mature, or the hand-stitching of full-grain leather, our philosophy is simple: If it cannot last a lifetime, we do not make it.
